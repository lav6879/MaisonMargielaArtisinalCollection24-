Creative Director: John Galliano

John Galliano is a British fashion designer renowned for avant-garde designs. Born in 1960, he gained fame for his flamboyant style in the 1980s. Galliano served as head designer at Givenchy and later became creative director at Christian Dior. Known for pushing boundaries, Galliano remains influential in the fashion world, celebrated for his theatrical and innovative approach.

Creative Direction: Margiela SS24

Creative Director John Galliano’s mesmerising collection examines the ritual of dressing as a physical expression of emotion. Dolls were a point of reference illustrated by the marionette-like way in which several models walked.

The collection was showcased under Pont Alexandre III in Paris with sinister smoke-under-the-bridge mis-an-scéne inspired by Brassai’s iconic photos of nocturnal Paris, transported the show's guests into the bohemian atmosphere of a Parisian evening accompanied by the sound of the Seine. The atmosphere was intimate and evocative. Set in an abandoned speakeasy, the collection was a theatrical exploration of the city’s underbelly, populated by characters who were both glamorous and deeply human.
